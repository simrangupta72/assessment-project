STYLEEN- Walk in for Fashion, Stay in for Style.

PROBLEM STATEMENT:
A personal shopper can help you discover what looks best on you and can help you
build a wardrobe of major pieces and accessories that reflect your personal style. There
are situations when a skilled personal shopper can make a real difference. However not
everyone has the time that they can spare to go meet there stylist in person. This is
where our website comes in role... You signup and register your preferences from where
you get assigned to your personal shopper online!

FEATURES:
Styleen is a dynamic styling solution that carefully crafts the desired looks as per your
personality, lifestyle, budget, and needs by your own Personal Stylists.
▪ Our service helps you save time and trouble of selecting clothing for yourself, by
conveniently delivering handpicked styles better, smarter and faster right to your
doorstep.
▪ The website provides you with a Sign-up option from where you are redirected to
your Style profile and give your basic details and preferred style.
▪ It also includes a login page for already registered customers and Google Login
Option.
▪ The main page provides you with a step by step instruction guide on how the
procedure works.
▪ It gives you a basic synopsis of what you should expect by our services and how
they would be catered and at what pricing.
▪ It also gives you a Google Map Integration that gives you contact details of your
Personal Stylist.
